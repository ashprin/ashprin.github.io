Prompt Insurance

When writing your About Us page, you don’t want to say the same things that appear on your Home page, and a long, detailed, historical summary will bore the reader. You also don’t want to sound like you’re boasting. As in most things, the best answer is to find the middle ground. Much like a resume you’d use to land a job, your About Us page is the place to toot your own horn. To avoid bragging or appearing pushy steer clear of hype. Instead offer a straightforward presentation of the facts and figures. Avoid superlatives.
